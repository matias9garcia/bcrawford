Broderick Crawford Labrín


I’m a Full Professor of Computer Science and currently Vice Dean of the Engineering Faculty at the Pontifical Catholic University of Valparaíso. I received my MBA at Universidad de Chile in 2001 and my PhD degree in Informatics Engineering from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, in 2011.

My areas of research interest include Artificial Intelligence, Metaheuristics, Constraint Programming, Operations Research and Software Engineering. In this context, I have published more than 300 scientific papers in different journals. I have also participated as leader and co-leader of an important number of national and international research projects.
